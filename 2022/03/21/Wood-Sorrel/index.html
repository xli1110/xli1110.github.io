Tips

Crazy Expansion

Wood sorrel has an explosive seed pod. Once touch on pinch, tens of thousands of seeds will explode everywhere, and those descents invade other plants’ occupation. However, this boom feeling addicted me in primary school, so I must help a lot of wood sorrels thrive and flourish. Anyway, extinguish this species ASAP, and never touch those pods. Otherwise, it will expand its territory madly.
