Tips

Shy Flower

Autumn crocus flowers look like shy ladies. They have a beautiful appearance but do not open completely while blooming, and each flower faded less than 1 week.

Colchicine

Colchicine, originally extracted from autumn crocus, can be extreme poisonous. Watch out while growing this plant, and never eat any part of it (bulbs are the most poisonous part).
