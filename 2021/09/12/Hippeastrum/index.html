Transplant

After 1-year’s growth, their bulbs’ size expanded twice as big as initially planted, and their roots filled all room in the old pots. Time to transplant. During transplanting, spent a big effort to drag them out of the old pots, since they were totally inserted into the containers like mosaic. Inevitably, hurt some mature leaves during dragging, but actually it did not matter. Focusing on the roots is the key in transplanting.

Tips

Rich Species

Genus hippeastrum has an incredible number of gardening species, and new varieties are created anytime. Pick up preferred species to grow.

Fertilizer

After flowers faded, fertilize NPK = 20:20:20 once or twice per 10 days. Then, they can easily bloom every year, unlike narcissus which are hard to re-bloom.
