Tips

Prune

Prune after Purchase


Prune ALL bulbs, new branches, and weak branches. Jasmine needs approximately 2 weeks to recover from the long delivery. During this period, blossom or growing new branches will consume a lot of energy, which may kill the plant.

Topping


Top each branch at the second or third pair of leaves. Then, new branches will grow from the axil.

Prune after Bloom


Prune the faded flowers ASAP. Prevent jasmine from producing seeds, which consumes a huge energy. To grow a new plant, start from a stem cutting, never considering seeds.

Fertilizer

When blooms, fertilize NPK = 10:30:20. Else, fertilize NPK = 20:20:20.

Infection

Jasmine is prone to infections. When so, isolate it from other plants.
