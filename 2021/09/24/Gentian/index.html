Light Sensitivity

Interestingly, mature gentian flowers open when there is sufficient light and close when not. Without artificial light, they open at noon and close at night.

Lignified Stem

Mature gentian stem gradually become lignified from bottom to top.

Ice-Cream and Star

From top view, gentian bulb looks like an ice-cream surrounded by a star.


Prune

When spring comes, prune dead lignified stems, which offer new stems more space to grow.

Time to Prune

Method

Cut the bottom of the stem. Left 3-5 cm for gripping. Then gripped the rest and pulled the dead stem out of the soil.

  • Cut

  • Rest

Result


Tips

Water

Water it when the soil surface becomes dry. Once losing water, stems and leaves will fade and can not recover. It is weird, such a small plant needs a big amount of water.

Temperature

Keep the temperature lower than 25 °C. Otherwise, the plant will be hurt and become soft and weak. On the other hand, gentian has perfect cold resistance. It can survive outdoors below -20 °C without any special care.

Sunlight

Do not expose gentian to full sun, and let it grow under other plants shade, like a tree. Otherwise, the stem and leaves will become weak and soft. When the highest temperature increases above 25 °C (typically at the middle of April in Beijing), move it under some shade instead of exposing to sunlight in a balcony.

Fertilizer

Gentian does not need a big amount of fertilizer. Be cautious, and avoid over fertilizing.
