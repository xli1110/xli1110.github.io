Tips

Temperature

Keep the temperature lower than 20 °C. When it becomes higher than 20 °C, freesia goes dormant. To decrease the temperature, can move it from full sun to partial shade, or move it away from indoor heat.

Dry Flower

It is convenient to make freesia dry flowers. After blossom, the flower’s shape shrank a bit, but the color remained. Surprisingly, the sweet odor also remained.
