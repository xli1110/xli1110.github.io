Black Nightshade

xli
2021-09-05
Plant

Classification

Class Order Family Genus
Eudicots Solanales Solanaceae Solanum

Basic Information

  • Flower Color: White and Yellow
  • Bloom Time: April - October
  • Native Area: Asia
  • Toxic: Yes

Evaluation

Item Score Note
Flower 8/10 Cute white star with a yellow core.
- - - - - -
Leaf 3/10 Common Eudicots leaves, soft and hairy.
- - - - - -
Odor 4/10 Slight bitter.
- - - - - -
Disease/Pest 5/5 All good.
- - - - - -
Easy to Grow 5/5 Hard to extinguish.
- - - - - -
Overall 25/40 OK, A common wild plant.

Log

  • Start Status: Seeds
  • Note: Accidentally mixed seeds into the soil to grow cosmos. Only take pictures of the flower and fruit.

2019 - 09




Tips

Crazy Growth

It grows from a seed to an adult in only 1.5 months, so lively and energetic. On the other hand, it nearly killed all cosmos grown in the same pot.

Fruit

Eatable when it turns into red, tastes so sour.
