Botanical Garden

Basic Information

  • Location: 990 Washington Ave, Brooklyn, NY, 11225
  • Time of Visit: 2019 - 02 - 01
  • Ticket Price: 0
    Visited this garden in a freezing day, about -35 °C, the receptionist kindly waived my ticket fee. I only found 2 other visitors that day.

Plant

Titan Arum

Due to its odor, like rotting corpse, it is also called the corpse flower.

It is the origin of a Pokémon, Vileplume.

Yellow Crane Flower

Natal Albuca

Many from this Genus have curvy spring-like leaves.

Bulbs grow from axil.

Honey Flower

Scientific name is Melianthus major, native to South Africa.

Found some brown sticky drops on a leaf, asked one of the staff. She assumed they are dirty water dropped from the roof. Another one said they are secretions of the honey flower. Tasted them, better than common honey.
