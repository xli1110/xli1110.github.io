Tips

Rich Species

Genus lilium has an incredible number of species. Color, odor, and bloom time, vary a lot. Pick up preferred species to grow.

Fertilizer

The bulb contains enough energy for the first blossom. If try to bloom next year, fertilize it frequently after bloom. TBH, this costs at least twice as much as buying new bulbs.

Stem Root

Beside of common basal root, members from Genus lilium also have stem root. Plant bulbs deep into the pot to avoid exposing stem root to the air.
