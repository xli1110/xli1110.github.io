Basic Information

Yellow Daffodil

  • Flower Color: Yellow
  • Bloom Time: April - May
  • Native Area: Europe
  • Toxic: Yes

Chinese Sacred Lily

  • Flower Color: White and Yellow
  • Bloom Time: January - February
  • Native Area: China
  • Toxic: Yes

Evaluation

Yellow Daffodil

Item Score Note
Flower 5/10 Common small yellow flowers.
- - - - - -
Leaf 6/10 Elegant and slim leaves with beautiful parallel veins, but easy to be leggy and spread out.
- - - - - -
Odor 3/10 No obvious smell, but when sniffing, there comes a slight bad smell making me sick.
- - - - - -
Disease/Pest 5/5 All good.
- - - - - -
Easy to Grow 5/5 Common soil, no need to fertilize.
- - - - - -
Overall 24/40 OK, common plant.

Chinese Sacred Lily

Item Score Note
Flower 7/10 White petals around yellow core, nice combination. Four flowers on each scape.
- - - - - -
Leaf 6/10 Elegant and slim leaves with beautiful parallel veins, but easy to be leggy and spread out.
- - - - - -
Odor 8/10 Fragrant, and fragrance spreads easily, filling the whole room. Feel comfortable and clear-headed, but it still contains an inappreciable bad smell.
- - - - - -
Disease/Pest 5/5 All good.
- - - - - -
Easy to Grow 5/5 Tap water plus sunlight.
- - - - - -
Overall 31/40 Pretty good.

Tips

Yellow Daffodil

Water

If grow indoor, do not water it until the soil is dry.

Chinese Sacred Lily

Perennial

Narcissus is a perennial plant, but this species only blooms once per two to three years and is easy to degenerate, so dump it after it fades.
