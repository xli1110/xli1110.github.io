Botanical Garden

  • Location: 4101 Rue Sherbrooke Est, Montreal, Quebec, H1X 2B2
  • Time of Visit: from 2017 to 2019, twice per month in summer and once in winter
  • Price: 0 (annual membership fee: 30 CAD)

Botanical Illustration

Gloriosa

Also named flame lily or climbing lily. Must have a high temperature to grow it. It can climb or scramble over objects, which is very rare in liliales, and mature flowers’ petals can turn backward.

Old Illustrations from 1950s

Insectarium

The garden also has an insectarium. Unlike other insect museums, the most attractive thing is its living insect chambers which exhibit various insects. Besides, it showcases a living atta ant kingdom which has attained the highest level of social organization on the 2nd floor with an announcer. Unfortunately, because of the strong light reflection on the glass wall, it is not easy to take a picture of living insects.

Activity

The Witch of Storytelling

Usually hosted 1 week before Halloween. Kids made some pumpkin carvings in the garden and listen to a witch telling stories. Unfortunately, she only talked in French.


Butterfly Festival

The garden hatches various butterfly eggs at the beginning of each spring, and show visitors the whole living life cycle of butterflies, including egg, larva, pupa, and adult. I have searched my phone, but can only find this image. Others are probably lost.
