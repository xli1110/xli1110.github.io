Preprocessing

Remove Invalid Timestamp

pandas can only handle timestamps from 1677-09-21 00:12:43.145225 to 2262-04-11 23:47:16.854775807, so if there are dirty timestamps in our data, like 2455-05-31, it is better to remove the sample (row) at first. Also, we convert timestamp to datetime in this step.

Remove Time Zone

Timestamps may be recorded in different time zones, and some of them may not have the time zone information. The subsequent time operations may suck if we do not unify various time zones. The simplest method is remove all time zones.
