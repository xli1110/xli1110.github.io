1 Common Angiosperms (Flowering Plants) Growing Log

List classification information of some flowering plants from class to genus. Usually, species is not considered (except for some distinguishable ones, like Citrus limon, commonly known as lemon), because the identification is too difficult and complicated, and some species are even controversial.
